GRAPHIC DESIGN

The Graphic Design program is designed to prepare students for freelance careers and/or entry-level employment in advertising agencies; printing and publishing firms; television and motion picture studios; advertising departments of large companies; commercial art studios; and desktop publishing, production art, graphic photography, electronic pre-press or offset printing and bindery operations. Students explore creative, technical, critical, and design-based solutions to real-world issues in order to meet the rigors of a dynamically evolving array of design professions.

How can I earn a certificate of achievement?

Complete the required graphic design courses and two elective courses.

How can I earn an associate degree?

Complete the general education degree requirements, the required graphic design courses, and two elective courses.

MULTIMEDIA DESIGN

The Multimedia program is an interdisciplinary program where students will engage in a wide range of integrated design disciplines. Students explore creative, technical, critical, and design-based solutions to real-world issues in order to prepare for freelance careers and/or entry-level employment in digital media, web design, UX/UI design, and interaction design.

How can I earn a certificate of achievement?

Complete the required graphic design courses and two elective courses.

How can I earn an associate degree?

Complete the general education degree requirements, the required graphic design courses, and two elective courses.
