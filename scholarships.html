ANNUAL SCHOLARSHIPS

Every spring, students are invited to submit their work to the Annual Graphic and Multimedia Design Scholarship Competition. The competition is an opportunity to share ideas, passions, and concerns about the world of design and multimedia. Students submit their work into the following categories: Advertising Design, Character Design, Digital Illustration, Editorial Design, Image Editing, Infographics, Motion Graphics, Time-based Design, Typographic Design, Packaging Design, Visual Identity, and UX/UI Design. All submitted work is displayed in the gallery.
