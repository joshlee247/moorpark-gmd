Multimedia: From Wagner to Virtual Reality

by Randall Packer and Ken Jordan

Publisher: W. W. Norton & Company; Expanded edition (December 16, 2002)

ISBN: 0393323757

This book is a must read for anyone interested in the history of multimedia, and where its going. The companion web site on www.artmuseum.net is a brilliant compliment to the book, with 50 video clips and rare photos.
