Conceptual Blockbusting: A Guide to Better Ideas

by James L. Adams

Publisher: Perseus Books Group; 4th edition (October 23, 2001)

ISBN: 0738205370

Very popular book with techniques and exercises to develop your problem-solving ability.

Creative Growth Games

by Eugene Raudsepp, George P. Hough Jr

Publisher: Perigee Trade (August 8, 1980)

ISBN: 039950415X

Mental gymnastics to exercise the brain. Good because of the informative text commenting on the games.
